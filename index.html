About Me

Welcome to my webpage! I am currently learning how to code through George Washington University's part-time coding boot camp, which runs from November 2017 until May 2018. I also work part-time as a recruiter at NPR, supporting the Programming and Digital Media departments. I have also worked as a math teacher at a public high school in the Bronx, and in the Human Resources department for DC Public Schools.

In my spare time, I love to practice yoga, go on hikes with my partner and our dog, listen to podcasts, and bike around the city. I am passionate about environmental, educational and equity issues.
